Open Source

What we build is open source.
Additionally we are using many other open source frameworks to build our product. It is in our belief and interest to contribute back to these projects whenever possible.

Quality

We believe in high quality software. That’s why we favor clean code: unit tested, peer reviewed, acceptance tested and documented. Our QA and build infrastructure is crucial to our success.

Innovation

We believe in the necessity for ongoing innovation. Most of the innovation at camunda comes from our own people. Slacktime and Ship-It days support innovation at camunda. Our community is another great source of inspiration.

Customer Focus

We believe that our users know what they need most. As with any other great product, many people have lots of ideas as to what should be done next. Some are great, some are inspiring, some are... It's the product owner's job to consolidate these ideas into our backlog.

Fun

Sometimes, new features, green jenkins and fixed bugs are not enough ;) Then we take a beer from our fridge and enjoy it on our comfy couch. We enjoy table soccer and Wii too. Maybe you will even see us laugh…
