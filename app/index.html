Our Vision

We believe in Business Process Management as the perfect instrument to create scalable business models.

We strive to create the best BPM platform in the world.

We think that the best BPM platform in the world must fulfill two top level requirements:

1st: Aligning Business and IT.

2nd: Enabling individual process solutions supporting individual business models.

This is what we build, and it is Open Source.
